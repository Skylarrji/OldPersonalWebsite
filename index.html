👋 Hi! I'm Skylar, a first-year Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo.

At the core of everything I do, I utilize technology, volunteering and my own experiences (the good and the bad) to change the way people think about and interact with themselves and those around them.

In the summer of 2022, I completed an internship at RBC as part of their Summer Tech Labs program, where I worked on an internal secure URL shortener using React, FastAPI, and MariaDB, projected to improve the experience for 85,000+ RBC employees. Some other notable projects I've completed in my spare time include a Christmas Countdown Chrome extension, a Python AI Battlesnake and an interactive recipe adjuster.

I believe the best way to create change is by taking initiative. This inspired me to found LEAP (Learning through Experiences And People) and United Under Arts Waterloo in high school. I also gave back to my community by being involved with SVP Teens, being a summer camp counsellor and donating 50 mask headbands to a local cancer centre.

In my spare time, I enjoy writing posts on LinkedIn and Medium in terms of my previous life experiences and what I've learned from them, especially topics such as insecurities, rejection, and mental health.

I'm open to giving advice and chatting with new people, so don't hesitate to reach out!
